An unconference about accessibility and people.

Accessibility Camp Seattle is a participant-driven meeting where sessions are proposed and decided upon by the group in person. We'll hold discussions, informal talks, and demos about accessibility and inclusive design in an effort to share knowledge and learn together.

Accommodations

To provide an inclusive event, we aim to have live transcription for all rooms. Please let us know within two weeks of May 18th if you need additional accommodations to participate, such as ASL interpreters.
